# Anomaly Alerts

Anomaly alerts fire automatically during nightly ingestion when metric thresholds are breached. They are sent as immediate Slack DMs — separate from the scheduled digest.

---

## Thresholds

| Alert | Condition | Severity |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| **CLS Spike** | CLS >2 standard deviations above your 7-day baseline | 🔴 High |
| **FDI Collapse** | FDI dropped >30% vs 7-day average | 🟡 Medium |
| **Recovery Misalignment** | RAS <0.4 for 3+ consecutive days | 🔴 High |
| **ML Anomaly** | Isolation Forest flags the day as anomalous | 🟡 Medium |

Thresholds use your **personal baseline** — not fixed numbers. Someone with consistently high CLS has a different normal than someone with consistently low CLS.

---

## Alert Format

```
⚠️ Presence Alert — Thursday 14 Mar

CLS SPIKE detected
Today: 0.89 (your 7-day avg: 0.31, threshold: 0.65)

Likely cause: 6 hours of meetings + 94 Slack messages
RAS: 0.41 — load is misaligned with recovery (WHOOP: 52%)

Recommendation: Tomorrow, protect at least 2 uninterrupted hours.
```

---

## Suppression

To avoid alert fatigue, a given alert type won't re-fire for the same type within 48 hours unless the threshold is exceeded by a larger margin.

---

## Implementation

`analysis/anomaly_alerts.py` — called from `DailyIngestionWorkflow` after metrics are written.
