# ML Models

Three scikit-learn models are trained on all accumulated JSONL data. They retrain automatically on the 1st of each month via the `MonthlyMLRetrainWorkflow`.

Models are stored in `data/models/` as `.pkl` files.

<Warning>
  Models require **≥60 days of data** to be statistically meaningful. Before that threshold, they run as warm-up baselines. Anomaly detection may fire false positives with limited history.
</Warning>

---

## Anomaly Detector — Isolation Forest

**File:** `data/models/anomaly_detector.pkl`

**Algorithm:** Isolation Forest (unsupervised)

**What it does:** Flags days where the combination of metrics is unusual vs your accumulated personal baseline.

**Features used:**
- CLS, FDI, SDI, CSC, RAS (daily averages)
- DPS
- Recovery score
- Day of week

**Output:**
- `anomaly_score` — the Isolation Forest contamination score
- `is_anomaly` — boolean flag

An anomaly is not necessarily bad — a remarkably good day is also anomalous. Context matters.

---

## Recovery Predictor — Random Forest

**File:** `data/models/recovery_predictor.pkl`

**Algorithm:** Random Forest Regressor

**What it does:** Predicts tomorrow's WHOOP recovery score from today's cognitive patterns.

**Features used:**
- Today's CLS, FDI, SDI, CSC, RAS
- Today's sleep hours and performance
- CDI (rolling debt)
- Day of week (weekend vs weekday)

**Output:** Predicted recovery percentage for tomorrow (0–100).

Used in the morning brief to flag "if you maintain today's pattern, tomorrow's recovery is projected at X%."

---

## Focus Clusters — KMeans

**File:** `data/models/focus_clusters.pkl`

**Algorithm:** KMeans (k=4)

**What it does:** Labels each day into one of four focus archetypes.

| Cluster label | Characteristics |
|--------------|----------------|
| **Deep Work** | High FDI, low SDI, moderate CLS, low CSC |
| **Meeting Heavy** | Low FDI, high SDI, high CLS, moderate CSC |
| **Recovery** | Low CLS across the board, low Slack volume |
| **Fragmented** | High CSC, variable FDI, mid CLS |

The cluster label appears in the weekly Intuition report and in the `data/summary/` rolling stats.

## Training

### Automatic retraining

`MonthlyMLRetrainWorkflow` runs on the 1st of each month at 02:00 Budapest. It:

1. Reads all available JSONL from `data/chunks/`
2. Extracts per-day feature vectors
3. Retrains all three models
4. Saves updated `.pkl` files
5. Logs training stats to Slack

### Manual retraining

```bash
# Train on all available data
python3 scripts/train_model.py

# Force train even with <60 days of data
python3 scripts/train_model.py --force
```
